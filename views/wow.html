The Problem

Roughly one third of the food produced in the world for human consumption is lost or wasted. Food waste not only amounts to a wasted resource, but also a costly one, amounting to $310 billion every year in developing countries. Consumers don’t purchase all the food available, leaving restaurants, supermarkets, and grocery stores to throw out “unattractive food”, throwing away food that is perfectly safe for consumption.

LEFTOVR's SOLUTION

LEFTOVR streamlines the process in which charitable organizations to access food donations, relieving the need to search for food donors and for institutions to donate to. Focusing on reducing food waste and providing a streamlined service to both food donors and charitable organizations—this platform aims to reduce food waste by also offering food to those in need, and providing a socially responsible way of clearing out “unsellable” food.

WHY DONATE?

By donating, food donors use an alternative way to clear out “unsellable” food, and also are reimbursed in advertising through LEFTOVR and it's partners—gaining positive PR through social change.
